# SpyderBot

**Understanding How AI Sees the World.**

SpyderBot is an AI Visibility Analytics and Intelligence platform helping organizations understand how AI systems mention, recommend, and cite brands, websites, products, and digital entities.

Our mission is to build the infrastructure layer for AI visibility.

## Focus Areas

- AI Visibility
- AI Search Intelligence
- AI Citation Analysis
- Entity Visibility
- Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)
